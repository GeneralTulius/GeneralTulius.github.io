New Ventures After Knee Injury

  Last night I tore my acl tendon and ruptured my meniscus. I was warming up and didn't feel any offputting sensations in my knee, so this was a complete shock to me. Needless to say my upcoming show will be cancelled as I begin recovring in the coming months. My meniscus tore right off the joint, so I will definately be needing a replacement. The problem is that, unfortunately, the current solutions don't last forever, so this means I will be needing to get my meniscus replace every 10-ish years. I will see what I can do with a knee like that. Maybe powerlifting makes more sense now...
